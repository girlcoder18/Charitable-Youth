About Us

About Charitable Youth for Nation Building Initiative

Charitable Youth for Nation Building Initiative is a community based Nigeria’s Non-Governmental Organisation. We are non-profit making and we are not political.We are focused on empowerment of the youths in the communities.

Learn More

Our Mission

To reach out to the youths in the society and ensure they participate in politics and government. At Charitable Youth for Nation Building Initiative, we believe that educated, employed, and engaged young people possess the power to solve the Nigeria’s toughest problems.

Our Vision

To attain a country where youth will fully participate in politics and government. An inclusive society that values and supports the full engagement of its young people in the civic, social, economic and cultural life of diverse and environmentally healthy communities.

Our Model

Our model is based on human resources development of people especially-the youths. The approach is centred on educational cum paternalistic. We gave training to youths who were too poor to attend full-time courses in the University. Top-down teaching methods are employed, but participants of our training are expected to make their own decisions about how to use the knowledge they acquire. We also disseminate the useful information through outreach activities and youth-to-youth exchanges.
