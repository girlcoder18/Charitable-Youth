REPORT ON THE RESUSCITATION OF THE GEORGE IWILADE LIBRARY

Following concern on the challenges faced by thousands of students from Iwoland in accessing educational resources for their academics and researches, Iwoland Development Coalition (IDC) in collaboration with the Charitable Youth for Nation Building Initiative (CYNBI), considered the resuscitation and upgrade of the George Iwilade Library as a step towards ameliorating that challenge.
